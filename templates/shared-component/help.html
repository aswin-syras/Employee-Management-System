An employee management system is a platform where all work-related as well as important personal details of an employee is stored and managed in a secure way. By using this system, you can manage admin activities in an easier and quicker way. Employees are the pillar of any organization and an ideal employee management tool makes a big difference to an organization.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How will I access the new system?

The new EMS is a cloud/web based system, so you can access anywhere you have an internet connection. You will use the URL to run the application. The new user will be added by the admin and username and password will be provided by admin. The password will be changed by clicking on forgot password.

2. Who do I contact if I forget my password?

On the login page, there is usually a forgot password link. You can click this link and follow the on-screen instructions. As a backup process, you can also contact admin and they will be able to reset your password to your default and then when you log on again, you will be prompted to change. If you do not have this link, contact your system administrator.

3. What if I need to change some of my personal information (i.e. banking information)?

On the user home page user can click on edit details icon, that will take them to edit user page. User can edit few details such as Personal details, Bank details, Skills and Qualities. To update the other information they need to contact to admin to get the details updated as they do not have rights to edit few details.

4. What is the purpose of employee management software?

Dedicated EMS helps in tracking and evaluating parameters like employee performance, goal tracking, attendance, and so on. The software brings all your data under one roof.

5. What do employee management systems do?

It helps you manage employee with easy tracking and storing of workforce information. The software keeps a track of data like leaves, punch records, performance, payments, reimbursements, etc.

6. What are the Benefits of an Employee Management System?

Benefits of an EMS are as follows- 1. HR data analytics 2. Compliant HR 3. Secured data 4. Full proof payroll 5. Keeps track of leave and attendance 6. Reduces paperwork 7. Saves HR time 8. Zeroed errors

7. How do new users can signup?

New users can be added by the admin. As Admin has rights and permissions to add new users.
